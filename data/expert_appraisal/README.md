# Expert appraisal data

This folder contains anonymized expert-appraisal materials and results for the digital shadow-puppetry interaction system.

The expert appraisal includes anonymized rating data and interview materials from six experts with different professional backgrounds. Expert identities are represented using coded IDs such as `E1`, `E2`, and so on.

## Contents

- `expert_appraisal_data_anonymized.xlsx`: anonymized expert rating data, interview materials, and summary results.

## Expert background categories

- E1: Senior technical executive of a high-tech enterprise
- E2: Practitioner in cultural tourism investment and strategic project evaluation
- E3: University faculty member in computer science and XR interaction
- E4: University faculty member specializing in educational technology
- E5: Director of a private education enterprise
- E6: National-level intangible cultural heritage inheritor / practitioner

## Data privacy

All expert-identifiable information has been removed. The released materials do not include expert names, phone numbers, emails, institutional identifiers, signatures, or other directly identifying information.

Interview materials are provided in anonymized form. Any references that could identify a specific person, institution, project, or organization have been removed or generalized.
